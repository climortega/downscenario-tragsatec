  • Accumulated hourly precipitation values (1, 3, 6, 12, and 24 hours) from reanalysis underestimate values obtained at AEMET observational stations, especially ERA5 and ERA5-Land, which have lower resolution.

  • This underestimation is most noticeable in extreme hourly precipitation values, exceeding 20%. CERRA and COSMO-REA6 show a high degree of bias.

  • Regarding the representation of intense precipitation events, CERRA and COSMO-REA6 simulate them in greater detail, but the underestimation remains significant, especially for daily data exceeding 100 mm.
