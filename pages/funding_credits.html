Funding and Credits

FUNDING

This project was funded through a service contract in which Tragsatec acted as a subcontractor for the Spanish State Meteorological Agency (AEMET).

The work was carried out between October 2024 and June 2026.

ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS

The researchers would like to thank the Climate Evaluation and Modeling Area at AEMET for their warm welcome and the availability of any resources that might have been needed.

Thanks are also due to Esteban Rodríguez Guisado for his meticulous supervision of the project; to Carlos Correa for his collaborative work on evaluation and development of the pyGridEval tool; to Alfonso Hernanz for his tireless assistance in both project conceptualization and pyClim-SDM software implementation; to Marta Domínguez, Sabela Sanfiz, and Miguel Hernández for their support, friendship, and help in applying our results to seasonal forecasting; to Toni Fernández Andrade for his invaluable IT and personal support; and to Ignacio Villarino and José Antonio Pellitero for their assistance in running AquaCrop.

Finally, the researchers would also like to thank AEMET for providing both material and IT resources, especially access to Cirrus, Ozonosfera, Criosfera and Espiga computers, as well as data from AEMET observational network and the applications from the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF).
