  • DeepESD downscaling method has been successfully applied to generate downscaled hindcasts, using the precipitation model (DeepESDpr) for this variable and the temperature model (DeepESDtas) for the others.

  • Downscaled hindcasts have been verified by evaluating extended winters (NDJFM, 3- and 5-month aggregations) from 1997 to 2016.

  • Temperature and wind show good Spearman correlation values.

  • Promising AUC and RPS skill indicators have been obtained for temperature.

  • Some predictive capacity has been achieved for wind and also, although less, for relative humidity.

  • Skill indicators could be improved using specific models for each variable.

  • Differences between aggregations depend on the variable.

  • These findings should be considered when studying downscaled forecasts for both 2024 and 2025.
